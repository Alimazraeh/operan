🗓️ Sprint Planning (Day 1 AM)
   ├─ PI Objective alignment
   ├─ Story refinement & estimation (Planning Poker)
   └─ Dependency mapping across pods

🔄 Daily Standup (15 mins)
   ├─ Pod-level sync
   └─ Cross-pod blockers escalated in Scrum-of-Scrums (3x/week)

🔍 Mid-Sprint Review (Day 6)
   ├─ Demo in-progress features
   └─ Adjust scope if integration risks emerge

✅ Sprint Review (Day 10 PM)
   ├─ Working software demo to stakeholders
   ├─ Acceptance criteria validation
   └─ Feedback incorporated into backlog

🧭 Sprint Retrospective (Day 11 AM)
   ├─ Process improvements
   └─ Technical debt prioritization
