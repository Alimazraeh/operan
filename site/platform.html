Design Principles

Built for Sovereign Operations

Multi-Tenant Isolation

Complete tenant separation at every layer. Data, processes, and outputs are cryptographically isolated per organization.

Sovereign by Design

Deploy within your own infrastructure. Full data residency controls. Air-gapped and offline-capable deployments.

Explainable First

Every output is traceable to its source. Every decision includes its rationale. No black-box operations.

Composable Architecture

Deploy modules individually or as a complete platform. Each module is independently versioned and upgradable.

Observability Built-In

Complete telemetry, audit trails, and performance monitoring across every module and every agent action.

Modular Runtime

Hot-swappable modules. Rolling updates. Zero-downtime deployments. Enterprise-grade reliability.
