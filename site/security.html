Security & Compliance

Sovereign by Design. Governed by Default.

Every security capability is built into the platform fabric — not layered on top. Six pillars of protection, eight governance mechanisms, complete auditability at every level.

Foundation

Six Security Pillars

Operan's security architecture is structured around six foundational pillars — each designed to meet the most demanding public-sector requirements.

Data Sovereignty

Complete control over where data resides, who accesses it, and how it flows. Tenant-isolated at every layer with cryptographic separation. Your data never leaves your infrastructure.

Private Deployment

Deploy entirely within your own data center or sovereign cloud. No third-party data processing. No external dependencies. Full operational independence from day one.

Air-Gapped Support

Operate in classified environments with full air-gapped deployment capability. Offline updates, isolated data pipelines, and complete network segregation support.

Complete Auditability

Every agent action, every decision, every data access — logged, timestamped, and immutable. Full audit trails support regulatory compliance and forensic investigation.

Human Approval Workflows

Critical decisions require human authorization. Configurable approval gates at every sensitive operation. Digital workers execute autonomously within defined boundaries.

Role-Based Access Control

Granular RBAC with multi-dimensional access controls. Role, department, project, and data-class based permissions. Every action validated at runtime.

Governance in Action

Human-Governed Autonomy

Operan's digital workers operate autonomously within defined boundaries — but critical decisions always require human authorization. This is not black-box automation.

Approval Workflow Engine

Threshold-based gates — Define risk thresholds automatically requiring human approval
Role-gated authorization — Only designated personnel can approve sensitive operations
Multi-step approvals — Sequential or parallel approval workflows for complex decisions
Full context provision — Humans receive complete rationale, evidence, and risk assessment
Audit trail preservation — Every approval or rejection is permanently logged

Explainability & Transparency

Decision traceability — Every agent output links to its source data and reasoning chain
Policy enforcement logging — All policy checks, passes, and denials are recorded
Model transparency — Know which model processed each request and why
Change management — Every configuration change is versioned and attributed
Real-time monitoring — Live dashboards showing agent activity, approvals, and policy violations
